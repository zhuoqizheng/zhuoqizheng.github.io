Gui­tar col­lec­tion, part 3

Posted at 2022-10

Cur­rent Elec­tric gui­tars

Fender Eric Clap­ton Stra­to­caster

First of all, with­out dis­cussing the CS or Lace Sen­sor ver­sions, let’s fo­cus on this bud­get​-​friendly ver­sion. The Tokai TLB​-​180, brand new and un­boxed, is priced at 17,000 HKD, dis­counted to 12,900 HKD. Sig­na­ture mod­els usu­ally don’t par­tic­i­pate in dis­count ac­tiv­i­ties, com­monly priced at 85% of the reg­u­lar price, while dis­play mod­els seem to be at 80%.
首先說，不討論CS，或者Lace拾音器版本，先關註這個乞丐版。通利全新開箱，標價17k，折後12900港幣，簽名款一般很少會參與打折活動，普遍標價85折，陳列品好像是8折。

Let’s start with the draw­backs:

  1. the vin­tage​-​style tone knob is­n’t very sta­ble, the sin­gle​-​ply pick­guard is prone to de­for­ma­tion, and get­ting used to the finer threads may take some time.
    先說缺點：復古調音旋鈕不夠穩定，單層護板容易變形，細小品絲需要重新習慣。

  1. Pay at­ten­tion to the de­tails; the Soft V​-​shaped neck is not much dif­fer­ent from a reg­u­lar C, suit­able for a rock​-​style grip, and it of­fers very com­fort­able sup­port. The neck is­n’t glossy, which is great as it does­n’t get sticky!
    需要留意的細節，soft V琴頸與普通C差別不大，適合搖滾手型，支撐非常舒服；琴頸不是光漆，不粘手這一點非常好！

  2. Key point!!!! The cir­cuit is very ver­sa­tile, and many peo­ple who find the sound not to their lik­ing might not be us­ing its cir­cuit prop­erly!
    重點！！！！！！電路非常豐富，很多人覺得聲音不好聽是不會使用它的電路！

Three knobs: the first one is Vol­ume, the sec­ond is Tone, and the third is Boost. If you set all three knobs to 10 when you pick it up, you’re do­ing it wrong.
三個旋鈕，第一個是音量Vol­ume，第二個音色Tone，第三個是激勵Boost。假如當你上手把三個旋鈕全打到10就錯了。

  • Vol­ume: At 10, it has a boost of about 10​-​12db com­pared to a reg­u­lar Strat, usu­ally used around 7 to match a reg­u­lar Strat.
    Vol­ume：10的時候相比於普通strat大概有10​-​12db的Boost，一般使用在7左右與普通Strat相同；

  • Tone: It has a TBX knob, gen­er­ally set at 5, equiv­a­lent to 10 on a reg­u­lar Strat, 0​-​5 is the same as a reg­u­lar Strat’s tone knob, and 5​-​10 can in­crease the high tones.
    Tone：TBX旋鈕，一般使用在5，與普通strat的10相同，0​-​5與普通strat音色旋鈕相同，5​-​10可以增加高音；

  • Boost: At 0, it’s the same as a reg­u­lar Strat, in­creas­ing the mid​-​fre­quency gain by about 12db from 0 to 10.
    Boost：0與普通strat相同，0​-​10增加中頻增益大概有12db左右。

So, to get a nor­mal Strat sound, set Vol­ume to 7, Tone to 5, and Boost to 0.
To get close to a fat hum­bucker tone, set Boost to 10, Tone to 5, and ad­just Vol­ume ac­cord­ingly.
所以，想要獲得正常Strat聲音，需要Vol­ume 7，Tone 5，Boost 0。
想要接近雙線圈肥厚音色，Boost 10，Tone 5，Vol­ume自行調整。

By us­ing these knobs in com­bi­na­tion, you can achieve a wide range of out­put com­bi­na­tions. For ex­am­ple, if you want a solo, you can pull up the Boost and set Tone to 10, achiev­ing a sound sim­i­lar to a Strat af­ter a light over­drive.
這幾個旋鈕配合使用，可以得到非常多的輸出組合。比如，想要solo的時候，可以不用踩額外踏板，把Boost拉起來，Tone也拉到10，可以得到類似於通過了輕過載後的Strat音色。
