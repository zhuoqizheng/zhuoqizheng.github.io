About

Zhuoqi

Researcher | Robotics and Machine Learning

I am a researcher with an academic and professional background in robotics and machine learning. Currently, I work as a Senior Engineer specializing in Applied Sciences.

我是一名專注於機器人與機器學習的研究員。目前，我擔任高級工程師，專注於應用科學。

I earned my Ph.D. from the Department of Biomedical Engineering at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU), where my research focused on autonomous mobile robots navigating complex social environments. My work contributed to new methodologies for robot perception, decision-making, and interaction in dynamic, real-world scenarios. Prior to this, I completed my Master of Engineering degree at the School of Electronics and Information Technology, Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), where I concentrated on unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) control and computer vision, achieving results that bridged theoretical innovation and practical application.

我在香港城市大學（CityU）生物醫學工程系獲得博士學位，研究方向為複雜社交環境中的自主移動機器人。我在博士期間的研究成果為機器人的感知、決策及互動提供了新方法，推動了機器人在動態真實場景中的應用發展。在此之前，我於中山大學（SYSU）電子與信息工程學院完成了工學碩士學位，專注於無人機（UAV）控制與計算機視覺研究，成功實現了理論創新與實際應用的結合。

In addition to my technical expertise, I am an avid amateur guitarist and actively pursue personal music projects. I enjoy blending various styles, including blues, rock, and metal, to create unique compositions. Music fuels my creativity, inspiring both my artistic and professional endeavors.

除了專業研究工作外，我還是一名熱愛音樂的業餘結他手，積極進行個人音樂創作。我擅長融合布魯斯、搖滾與金屬等多種音樂風格，創作出富有個性與創意的作品。音樂不僅豐富了我的業餘生活，也啟發了我的創新思維。

I am passionate about innovation and collaboration in the fields of robotics, machine learning, and intelligent systems. I welcome discussions on related projects, partnerships, and opportunities to push the boundaries of technology together.

我熱衷於機器人、機器學習與智慧系統領域的創新探索，並期待與志同道合的專業人士共同推動技術的邊界。歡迎交流、合作與項目探討。
