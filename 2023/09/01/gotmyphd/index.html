Fi­nally, I got my Ph­D

Posted at 2023-09-01

Com­plet­ing a Ph­D has been a chal­leng­ing yet re­ward­ing jour­ney. In this blog post, I will share the key stages of my fi­nal phase.

Oral Ex­am­i­na­tion (Mid​-​Sep­tem­ber):

Con­sist­ing of a 40​-​minute con­cise pre­sen­ta­tion and a 90​-​minute ques­tion​-​and​-​an­swer ses­sion, the viva pro­vided an en­light­en­ing ex­pe­ri­ence that en­riched my un­der­stand­ing of the topic.

Mi­nor Re­vi­sion (Sub­mit­ted in mid​-​Oc­to­ber):

Fol­low­ing the viva, a mi­nor mod­i­fi­ca­tion process was un­der­taken. I re­fined my pa­per based on con­struc­tive feed­back to en­sure it met the aca­d­e­mic stan­dards.

Fi­nal Sub­mis­sion (De­cem­ber):

Af­ter a pro­longed wait for de­fense com­mit­tee and fac­ulty re­view, the jour­ney cul­mi­nated in the fi­nal sub­mis­sion, mark­ing the end of my Ph­D ca­reer.

Af­ter sev­eral years, the jour­ney fi­nally came to an end. I re­cently came across this sen­tence:

Not every­one is ca­pa­ble of do­ing a Ph­D.”

Sad but true.
